Layer norm¶

Layerwise normalization…

After computing the layer output, W_t * h_t, get vector x_t

Compute the mean and variance of it then

\[x_t = \frac{g}{\sigma} * (x_t - \mu) + b\]

Where g and b are 2 trainable vectors
