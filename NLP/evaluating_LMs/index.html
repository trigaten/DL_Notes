Evaluating LMs¶

BLEU¶

Compute precision of predicted sentence’s 1-4grams compared to true translation(s)

\[BLEU = min(1, \frac{output length}{reference length})(\Pi_{i=1}^{4}precision_i)^{1/4}\]
